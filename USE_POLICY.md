# Use Policy

This project aims to support Telugu language technology responsibly.

## Allowed Use

- Education and research
- Developer productivity
- Accessibility and language inclusion
- Non-harmful automation and assistance

## Prohibited Use

- Harassment, abuse, or hate content generation
- Disinformation campaigns
- Privacy violations or unlawful surveillance
- Malware creation or cyber abuse
- Academic cheating at scale without disclosure

## Safety Expectations

- Evaluate models for harmful outputs before release.
- Clearly label synthetic content where appropriate.
- Implement safeguards for high-risk use cases.
- Provide a channel for reporting misuse or harms.

## Governance

Maintainers may restrict, deny, or remove contributions that violate this policy.
